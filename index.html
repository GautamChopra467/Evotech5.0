ABOUT EVOTECH 5.0

EVOTECH , launched with a vision to let the young developers portray their products solving real-world problems and inspire others.

There are three parallel tracks for project submissions - Evolve, Innovate & Amplify. The event is held in two stages:

    1. Summary and Details of the project (Online) &
    2. Exhibition and Competition (Offline)

* Both Software and Hardware projects are allowed.

GUIDELINES

  • Each team can have maximum 4 members and minimum 2 members
  • Teams have to bring all the software and hardware components required by them to pitch up the project themselves.
  • Every team would be given 2 hours to set up their project and 3 hours for the exhibition.
  • Teams have to build a working prototype for exhibition otherwise they will not be judged in the competition.
  • At the end of the exhibition each team would be given 10 mins for their demonstration to the judges.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

  • Students of all undergraduate and post graduate courses from Delhi-NCR colleges are welcome to participate in this event.
  • All the team members have to be from same college.
  • Students can present projects from any discipline of software and hardware.
  • There is no restriction on the genre of the projects.

HOW TO REGISTER

  • Teams have to register themselves online at the given website under register section.
  • All teams have to build their project before the competition/ exhibition and submit a short description while registering online for the event.
  • Format for the description is as follows :

    • TITLE (PROJECT NAME AND COLLEGE NAME)
    • INTRODUCTION (PROBLEM AND SOLUTION)
    • TECHNOLOGIES INVOLVED
    • COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS TO SIMILAR EXISTING TECHNOLOGY
    • FEASIBILITY AND APPLICATIONS
